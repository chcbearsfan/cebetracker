What is CEBE?

CEBE (Common Equity Bitcoin Exposure) is a framework for analyzing Bitcoin Treasury Companies that reveals what percentage of Bitcoin on the balance sheet is actually owned by common equity holders after accounting for senior claims.

CEBE = (Total BTC - Senior Claims in BTC) √∑ Shares Outstanding

Drag = Senior Claims √∑ Total BTC

Why it matters: A company with 10,000 BTC and 22% drag means common equity holders own 78% of that Bitcoin. As BTC price rises, senior claims (denominated in fiat) compress in BTC terms, increasing common equity's ownership percentage over time.

This dynamic explains why Bitcoin Treasury Companies can outperform direct Bitcoin ownership‚Äîthe compression of senior claims creates amplification beyond simple leverage.
